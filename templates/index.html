Recipe Generator

How does this work?

Writing recipes can be really dull, so this tool was made to streamline the process, so you can focus on making and sharing good food. Just type what you're making, the ingredients you used, and optionally some rough steps on how to make the dish, or press the mic button and say it aloud. And voila, a beautifully formatted recipe you can save, share and make time and time again!
